What are your hours?

Our office is open weekdays from 6:00AM – 3:00PM, Pacific Standard Time.

How can I reach you?

Please call us at 1-800-399-2096 when our office is open. If you’d like to contact us at other times, please use our contact form. We will contact you first thing in the morning on the next business day.

What types of loans and services do you provide?

We specialize in providing small business loans for business owners who have trouble getting traditional loans and funding from banks. Polaris Capital Group has a network of lenders that allows us to provide business loans for people with bad credit and business loans with no money down – we offer unique options where you can use your current business assets for collateral or take out a line of credit against your home. Please see our Product Highlights page for more information about our specific financial services for small business owners.

My business is brand new. Can I still secure a small business loan with Polaris?

Absolutely – if you have a W-2 of at least $100K and a FICO score of at least 660, we are happy to work with you to secure a small business loan.

What if I have limited or bad credit?

Polaris Capital Group recognizes that even for those with bad credit, small business loans are and should be possible. Our team of expert Business Loan Consultants will work with you to find a way to get the funding you need. The lenders we work with are able to offer high risk business loans because we first determine the best financial service for each individual client. We also offer guaranteed credit repair services, where one of our Consultants goes through each line of your credit report with you and helps you make a plan to improve your credit.

What is needed to begin the loan process?

For most loans, you will start by completing an application with one of our consultants. We will need your bank statements for the past 6 months, a photo ID, business address and website URL, personal and business tax schedules, a cancelled check, and wire instructions for your loan funds.

How long will the loan process take?

For most loans, we can secure approval and disburse your funds within 6-72 hours.

How much can I borrow through Polaris?

On average, our loans range from $20K to $2 million.

If I hire Polaris to repair my credit, how long until my score improves?

It depends. Most of our clients start seeing higher credit scores in 35 days to 6 months.
