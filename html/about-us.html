About Us     

Polaris Capital Group

Polaris Capital Group provides a comprehensive range of personal and business financial services. Our goal is to provide the right funding solution for all small business financial needs with an outstanding customer service experience.

We provide business loan services and options to small businesses that are typically underserved by banks. Obtaining funding through big banks can be challenging, and when you’ve already put so much into your business, being told “no” is disheartening. That’s where Polaris comes in. We have a range of alternative funding options that can meet your needs. From credit repair services to private business loans, Polaris Capital Group has the experience, knowledge, and resources to find you the right funding, fast.

Our team strives to provide great funding options for nearly any financial need.

1 Depending on the current state of your business and your industry, you may benefit most from a working capital business loan, or a real estate-backed loan. Whether you need fast cash or are looking for a lower interest rate, our range of financial services, coupled with our consultative approach, guarantee that you’ll get the financial support you need.

2 We know that time is important to small businesses. It is important to us too, so we work fast. Every day you spend waiting for a business loan to be approved and funded is a day you’re not able to grow your business.

Instead of the typical 45-60 day waiting period required by traditional institutions, we offer express business loans that can be funded in just days. Faster funding means that business owners can get back to growing their businesses and achieving their goals. We work faster because we have minimal credit requirements and a growing team of in-house underwriters who are committed to making sure you get the right loan product for your needs. Even if you’ve been turned down by banks before, Polaris can help you get that critical cash your business needs.

3 Polaris Capital Group is proud to be located in San Diego, California. Being in one of the best cities to launch a start-up, according to Forbes, means we have plenty of opportunities to help small business owners secure the private business loans they need to help their business grow, succeed, and thrive. We are also proud to offer a wide variety of options of private business loans California businesses seek as an alternative to traditional banking institutions.

GIVING BACK


Aside from helping small businesses succeed to drive and grow our local economy, Polaris believes in giving back to our community by getting involved with local charities and non-profits. We are proud to work with It’s All About the Kids here in San Diego, California. It’s All About the Kids Foundation is a non-profit organization that creates new and innovative programs to benefit children’s charities and enhance the lives of less fortunate children and their families. Partying for a Purpose produces special events and fundraisers for the It’s All About the Kids Foundation. It’s All About the Kids Foundation and Partying for a Purpose are committed to the advancement of children through the joining of music, the arts and the generosity of corporations and individuals.
