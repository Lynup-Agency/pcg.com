Our Story     

Polaris Capital Group’s founder, Chad Schwartz, started in the financial industry brokering real estate loans. In 2011, he saw an opportunity to help small business owners who needed cash – fast – to grow their business. Traditional banks and lending institutions were unable to serve customers in need of commercial business loans because of loan restrictions and limited offerings.

Background In Business

With his background in business, Chad knew that the financial services small businesses and start-ups need are not “one size fits all.” Some need fast cash to purchase more equipment so they can expand to meet customer needs. Others don’t yet have the credit or equity to secure a traditional commercial business loan. They may have already secured a loan, but would like to lower their interest rates. Some just need a consultant to go through their credit report and provide strategies to improve it.


Making It Simple

Small business owners just looking for a simple, easy business loan were attempting to work with multiple institutions and various consultants or representatives, spending time traveling all over town trying to secure credit, loans, or consultations, and enduring lengthy waiting periods for loan approvals – instead of running and growing their business. Chad saw an opportunity to provide speed and simplicity and make the process of securing business loans easy.


All From One Company

With a vision to provide clients with any needed small business financial service, all from one company, Chad founded Polaris Capital Group. He built a team of knowledgeable financial consultants who shared the same drive to use their expertise to help small business owners. Business owners enjoy working with dedicated, passionate consultants and Polaris Capital Group enjoys helping businesses grow and succeed with the best service for business loans, guaranteed.
